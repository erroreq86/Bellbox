The Programme

Built for a ship's Wi-Fi.

Every part of Portholes is accessible with your private device and the company's CAT devices, tablets, and computers.

Shuffled every attempt

Questions and answer order randomise on every run, so no two attempts look alike.

Crew ID sign-in

Your six-digit crew ID unlocks each test and ties every score straight to the department roster.

Cloud leaderboard

Your best attempt syncs to a live leaderboard the moment you submit — only the top score counts.

Works without signal

Lost connection mid-ocean? Assessments fall back to local scoring and sync the moment you're back online.

Tamper-resistant scoring

Answer keys are obfuscated client-side, so results reflect what you actually know, not a shared screenshot.

Instant results

See your score the second you finish — no waiting on a supervisor to mark anything by hand.
